PAINT ROCK is my weird, care-worn warehouse of solo music. I’ve been in here with a forklift moving around pallets since age 18. I’m not forklift certified, and I don’t know how to get out.

Herein you’ll find half-baked and fully-baked ideas, from voice memos to fully mixed-and-mastered tracks with half a dozen collaborating artists. It’s a family-photo-album, a record of my growth, experiments, and excursions as an artist.

Looking for some easy-listening indie? Check out False Jolene or Epilogue: Pokemon Emerald Version

Looking for a composer to score your video game or short film? Check out Tears of a Greenland Shark or Theme (For Emma Kate’s Film)

Open to experimental storytelling? Check out story. or Havana, Backrooms

The warehouse is open- so rummage around with an open mind. You never know what you’ll find down here. Frankly, even I’ve forgotten.
