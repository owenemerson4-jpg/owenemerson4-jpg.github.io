“Midnight Snack”

A kitschy animation created as a visual accompaniment to my song “story.”. Created in Adobe After Effects from hand-drawn assets. Featuring original music.

“Lucy Leaves Town”

A nostalgic low-poly animation with interactive audio or visuals. Featuring original music. As the user scrolls their cursor further from the Earth, Lucy flies out into deep space, revealing a constellation of rainbow-colored stars and extraterrestrial sounds. This is achieved using mouse tracking mapped to the opacity of 3D objects and the frequency cutoff of a bandpass filter.

Processing Mini-Sketches

A series of artworks created using Processing, a coding language and platform designed for visual artists

^Houses.pde, a generative art program that creates random abstract compositions based on the colors of Georges Braque’s “Houses at l’Estaque”

^Finding Flowers.pde, an interactive game that generates minimalist “fields” and allows the user to scroll the mouse over the canvas to discover its hidden flowers

^PAINTROCK.pde, a low-poly animation depicting my artist logo: a house on a moonlit night.
